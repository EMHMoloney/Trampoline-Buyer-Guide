Choosing the right trampoline for your family.

> >

When choosing which size trampoline to buy, it's important to consider the size of your garden, and the ages of all family members who will be using it.


Trampoline shape does matter when it comes to home use . Round trampolines have a relatively 'softer' bounce, and are recommended for younger children. Rectangular and square trampolines have a relatively 'harder', more reactive bounce. They are recomended for older teen, and adult use. That is why you see trained, professional gymnasts using rectangular trampolines.The rectangular trampolines in public play zones (e.g. "JumpZone") have been heavily modified for public use.


When deciding, remember that all trampolines should have 3ft (1m) of space all around , to allow for access and safe play.
